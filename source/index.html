Outside The Box // A Creativity Game

Outside the box is a game that provides you with different tasks to encourage your creativity. The box contains 52 different cards with various creative tasks. To achieve maximum creativity, we recommend that you take at least one card each day.

The only rule you should follow is that you always try your best to fulfill the task and that you respect the process. Some of the tasks might take less than one hour and others might take the whole day.

You can play on your own or together with others. This game is recommended for human beings from the age of 15.

The founders of this game are very interested in following your journey. Take a picture of your creative result and post it on social media. Use #outsidethebox.
